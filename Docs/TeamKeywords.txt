


=== ally ===
A noun that is on the same team as this noun
A noun that is not on any team is not an ally

=== enemy ===
A noun that is on a team that is not the same team as this noun
A noun that is not on any team is not an enemy

=== nonally ===
A noun that is not on the same team as this noun
A noun that is not on any team is a nonally

=== own ===
An object that is owned by this player,
An object that is owned by the same player as this object,
A player that is this player,
A player that owns this object
An object that is not owned by any player is not included in this
